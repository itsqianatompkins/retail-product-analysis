# Background
Grind & Brew Coffee Co. is a multi-country specialty coffee retailer offering brewed beverages, espresso drinks, and packaged coffee in various roast profiles and sizes, catering to both casual customers and loyalty program members.

As the company scaled, it adopted a data-driven approach to optimize pricing, product mix, inventory, and customer engagement. This report analyzes transactional retail data to identify key revenue drivers, customer preferences, pricing efficiency, and inventory dynamics to support smarter business decisions.

# Overview

 - Identify which coffee types, roast profiles, and product sizes drive the highest revenue while distinguishing between revenue leaders and volume-driven items.
 - Evaluate average order value (AOV) across customers and regions and determine which products generate the strongest revenue per unit sold.
 - Assess whether higher-priced items enhance profitability or suppress demand and identify top-performing combinations of coffee type, roast, and size.
 - Analyze differences in product preferences by country and compare purchasing behavior between loyalty card members and non-members.
 - Measure the share of transactions attributed to loyalty program members and evaluate how loyalty participation influences purchasing patterns.
 - Track units sold over time to assess demand consistency and identify fast- versus slow-moving products using sales velocity.
 - Determine which coffee types and roasts should be prioritized for production and identify seasonal demand patterns by roast profile or product size.
